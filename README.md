# Industrial Armies for Neoforge

An original NeoForge 1.21.1 military logistics mod. The first playable milestone implements
army enlistment and field orders; subsequent milestones connect manpower to MineColonies and
equipment production and logistics to Create.

## Alpha gameplay

1. Craft an Enlistment Contract and carry at least 16 emeralds.
2. Use the contract on a village bell to enlist a Company Soldier.
3. Soldiers use the vanilla villager model, follow their owner, and fight according to their stance.
4. Use a Command Baton on the ground to order nearby soldiers to hold that position.
5. Use the baton in the air to recall nearby soldiers into follow mode.
6. Use the baton directly on one soldier to toggle only that soldier's order.
7. Sneak-use the baton on one soldier to open its individual settings screen. You can edit its
   name, order, combat stance, follow distance, and name visibility.

Vanilla villager and item textures are development placeholders. No code or assets from
Villager Recruits are included.
